Targets from pro benchmarks — knee bend at loading: 130–150° · hitting elbow at trophy: ≈ 90° · arm at contact: fully extended, ≈ 170–180° · toss line should stay just inside the baseline-side of your front foot.

How to use ServeLab
Weekly loop: film one side-view serve and check it in Analyze against the phase checkpoints → pick your weakest phase and its drill from this tab → hit a basket of 40–60 serves logging every one in Log → watch the trend in Progress. Change one thing at a time; re-film weekly.
